Welcome to Kinetic Meditation

What is this web about?

This is a study about understanding how you would interact with an online meditation. The meditation is called Mahasati Meditation.

What are you going to do?

You will be asked to follow a series of meditation poses (total of 14 poses) based on the Mahasati meditation. There will be some questionnaires before and after you do the practice.

What will happen?

We will need access to your camera, then an image will show up, please follow the image pose.


The data from your camera feed will be sent to TensorFlow PoseNet, a Machin Learning model to estimate your pose by estimating the spatial locations of key body joints in real-time. We do not collect or save your video.

What do you need?

A camera, a room to sit and move your hands (you will only be asked to move your hands - donâ€™t worry) so please make yourself comfortable as possible. Our previous participants mention that they need about 1 m away from the camera to show at least their upper part of the body.

Then what?

Fill the demography survey, follow the poses and fill some questionnaires about mindfulness, emotion and user experience.
